Mission

Home Protection

Discover the flood risk of your home and neighboorhood and take action to protect your family.

Infrastructure Investment

Identify high-risk areas for civic leaders and prioritize infrastructure development.

Disaster Planning

Invest in communities most at risk of the effects of climate change.

Data

Floodwise leverages NYC Open Data from NYC Department of IT and Telecommunications and the United States Census Bureau to develop insights into flood risk. Learn more by selecting the learn more button or launch the demo to experience floodwise insights.
